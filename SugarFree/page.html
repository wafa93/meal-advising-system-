Sugar-free foods.

A diet with a low sugar content improves overall health and weight loss. Avoid processed foods with a high sugar content. Here are some sugar-free foods:

  1. Meat:
    • beef
    • chicken
    • lamb
    • veal
    • rabbit
    • turkey
    • salmon
    • tuna
  2. Non-starchy vegetables:
    • avocados
    • artichokes
    • beets
    • mushrooms
    • tomatoes
    • kale
  3. Dairy products:
    • milk
    • cheese
    • yogurt
  4. Nuts:
    • almonds
    • hazelnuts
    • walnuts


Sugar damage.

It is recommended that the amount of calories consumed from added sugar is less than 10% per day, as sugar consumption is believed to be the main cause of obesity and many chronic diseases such as diabetes.:

  • Sugar damage:
    • Heart disease
    • Obesity
    • Depression


Nutritional facts about sugar.

Sugar is a food that provides the body with empty calories, which means that it does not have any nutritional value. A teaspoon of sugar, approximately 4 grams, contains::

Content Quantity
calories 16 g
Protein 0 g
fat 0 g
Carbohydrates 4.2 g
